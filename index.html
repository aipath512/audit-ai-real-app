AI Visibility — Can AI include your company in answers?

AI Visibility is the ability of AI systems to find, understand, trust and use information about your company.

AI Discovery — Can AI find you?

AI Discovery means AI crawlers can find your machine-readable entry points.

Measured by: robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt.

AI Understanding — Can AI understand what you do?

AI Understanding means your content is structured clearly enough for AI systems to classify your business.

Measured by: title, meta description, H1, JSON-LD, Organization Schema, Service Schema.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization

AIO — AI Optimization

AIO is the broader discipline of making a company understandable, usable and trusted by AI systems.

AI Signals — Machine-readable business signals

AI Signals are declarative files, headers, schemas and proof tokens that tell machine systems how to read, trust and use your website.

  4. Verifiable: Can AI verify proof and provenance?
