Business Glossary

Plain-language definitions for firm owners. This section is visible in HTML and also supported by DefinedTerm schema for AI systems.

AI Visibility — Can AI include your company in answers?

AI Visibility is the ability of AI systems to find, understand, trust and use information about your company.

5thelement.ai example: exposes AI-readable discovery, declaration, trust and proof signals.

Related: AEO, GEO, AIO, AI Discovery, AI Trust.

AI Discovery — Can AI find you?

AI Discovery means AI crawlers can find your machine-readable entry points.

Measured by: robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt.

5thelement.ai example: /robots.txt, /sitemap.xml, /llms.txt.

AI Understanding — Can AI understand what you do?

AI Understanding means your content is structured clearly enough for AI systems to classify your business.

Measured by: title, meta description, H1, JSON-LD, Organization Schema, Service Schema.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization

AEO helps AI systems answer user questions using your company information.

Business question: Can AI answer with my company?

Related: FAQ Schema, intents.json, AI Answer Readiness.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

GEO helps generative AI describe your company accurately.

Business question: Can AI explain who we are correctly?

Related: Entity Graph, AI Declaration, authority.json.

AIO — AI Optimization

AIO is the broader discipline of making a company understandable, usable and trusted by AI systems.

Business question: Can AI understand and trust our company?

AI Signals — Machine-readable business signals

AI Signals are declarative files, headers, schemas and proof tokens that tell machine systems how to read, trust and use your website.

Examples: llms.txt, ai.json, authority.json, intents.json, ai-proof.json.

llms.txt — AI directory file

llms.txt is a plain-text guide that helps AI systems understand the most important parts of a website.

5thelement.ai example: https://5thelement.ai/llms.txt

ai.json — Machine-readable company declaration

ai.json declares who the company is, what it offers and how AI systems should understand it.

authority.json — Authority declaration

authority.json helps AI systems identify the owner, operator and authority structure of a domain.

intents.json — Questions your company can answer

intents.json declares the buyer questions, use cases and business intents your company is prepared to answer.

ai-proof.json — Proof and provenance layer

ai-proof.json exposes proof references such as SHA-256 hashes, OpenTimestamps or Bitcoin anchoring.

ADI — AI Delivery Infrastructure

ADI is infrastructure that helps AI systems discover, understand, trust and act on company information.

5thelement.ai example: Edge-native AI-readable business infrastructure.

AI Visibility Spectrum — How AI sees a website
  1. Crawlable: Can AI bots access the site?
  2. Readable: Can AI extract meaning?
  3. Citable: Can AI use the site as a source?
  4. Verifiable: Can AI verify proof and provenance?
  5. Actionable: Can AI agents execute workflows?
